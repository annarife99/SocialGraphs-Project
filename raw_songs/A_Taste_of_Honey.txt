A version from this time was released in 1977 on the album ''[[Live! at the Star-Club in Hamburg, Germany; 1962]]''. As the instrumental version by Acker Bilk was popular in the United Kingdom at the time, the song was chosen to be recorded for their 1963 debut album Please Please Me, with Paul McCartney singing lead \u2014 this version is notable in that, during the middle eight, McCartney's vocal is double-tracked. the first of many songs in which the Beatles did so. In the United States this song first appeared on the [[Veejay Records]] album ''[[Introducing... The Beatles]]''. The Beatles also performed \"A Taste of Honey\" seven times for the BBC radio shows, including \"Here We Go\", \"Side by Side\" and \"Easy Beat\".
